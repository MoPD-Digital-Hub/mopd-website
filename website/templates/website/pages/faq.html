Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers about ministry updates, publications, procurement, vacancies, statistics, and contacting MoPD.

Where can I find official MoPD documents?

Use the Climate Documents and Statistics Documents pages to browse, preview, and download published PDF documents.

Can I download documents from the website?

Yes. Document library items include preview and download actions where the source file is available.

Where are procurement notices published?

Active tender notices are listed on the Procurement page. Expired notices are hidden from the public list.

How can I check current job openings?

Visit the Vacancies page for current openings, deadlines, locations, and downloadable vacancy files.

How do I contact the Ministry?

Use the Contact page to send a message or find the ministry phone, email, address, and map location.

How soon will I receive a response?

Response times depend on the inquiry type and office workload. Please include clear contact details and enough context for the team to route your message.

Does the website support Amharic and English?

Yes. Use the language buttons in the header to switch between available site languages.

What should I do if I find an accessibility issue?

Please report accessibility barriers through the Contact page so the website team can review and improve the experience.
