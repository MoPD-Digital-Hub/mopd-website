About MoPD

Driving Ethiopia's
Transformation

Welcome to the official website of the Ministry of Planning and Development (MoPD) in Ethiopia.

As the primary government institution responsible for national planning and development, the MoPD plays a vital role in shaping Ethiopia's sustainable growth and socio-economic transformation.

Through this website, we provide comprehensive information about the MoPD's initiatives, projects, and services — including valuable resources, reports, and data related to our national development agenda.

Monitoring & Evaluation

Data-driven assessment of development outcomes

Our mission is to provide comprehensive policy analysis, guidance, and support to drive the multidimensional development endeavors of our country.

We aim to play a pivotal role in shaping Ethiopia's future through robust public investment administration, efficient appraisal and prioritization of development projects, and rigorous monitoring and evaluation practices.

Our mission is grounded in transparency, accountability, and good governance — contributing to sustainable and inclusive growth that enables Ethiopia to thrive globally.

"Through strategic direction and effective policy guidance, we enable Ethiopia to thrive in an ever-changing global landscape."

— Ministry of Planning and Development

Our vision is to make Ethiopia an African beacon of prosperity, where the aspirations and dreams of its people are realized.

We envision a future Ethiopia that stands as a shining example of sustainable economic growth, social harmony, and environmental stewardship — where poverty and inequality are significantly reduced.

Together, we will transform Ethiopia into an African beacon of prosperity, where progress, innovation, and the well-being of its people are at the forefront.

Our Areas of Work

Six pillars driving Ethiopia's coordinated development agenda

01

Stakeholder Engagement

Coordinating with government agencies, international organizations, civil society, and the private sector.

02

Knowledge Sharing

Facilitating best practices across sectors to encourage effective development project implementation.

03

Policy Analysis

Comprehensive guidance on economic, social, demographic, and environmental development policies.

04

Evaluation & Monitoring

Periodic assessments of development plans to identify areas of improvement and ensure effectiveness.

05

Project Appraisal

Reviewing, selecting, and prioritizing public development projects aligned with national goals.

06

Project M&E

Monitoring and evaluation of public development projects to ensure successful implementation.

In Focus

10-Year Development Plan

2021 — 2030

The 10-Year Development Plan outlines Ethiopia's vision, objectives, and strategic pillars for sustainable development — addressing economic growth, poverty reduction, sectoral development, energy, innovation, and human resources.

Its goals: improve living standards, access to services, gender inclusion, and transform the economy through human capital development and private sector involvement.
