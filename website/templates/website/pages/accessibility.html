Accessibility Statement

MoPD is committed to making this website accessible to all users, including people with disabilities.

We aim to follow recognized web accessibility guidelines, provide keyboard navigation, readable contrast, and bilingual content in English and Amharic.

If you encounter barriers using this site, please contact info@mopd.gov.et so we can improve your experience.
