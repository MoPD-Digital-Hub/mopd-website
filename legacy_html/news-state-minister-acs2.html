Ethiopia's State Minister Calls for United African Leadership Ahead of ACS2

During the Africa-wide stakeholder information session for the upcoming Second Africa Climate Summit (ACS2), Ethiopia's State Minister for Planning and Development, H.E. Mr. Seyoum Mekonen, called for bold, unified African leadership in tackling the climate crisis.

Speaking to over 450 stakeholders from regional institutions, academia, research organizations, and partners across the continent, he described ACS2 — set for 8–10 September 2025 in Addis Ababa — as "Africa's COP," a platform to showcase homegrown climate solutions.

"Africa is not waiting to be saved—it is leading with solutions," he said, highlighting Ethiopia's Green Legacy, Kenya's renewable energy leadership, and the Great Green Wall while stressing the urgent need to reform global climate finance systems.
