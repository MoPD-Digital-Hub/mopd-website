Ethiopia and France to Collaborate on the Second Africa Climate Summit

Ethiopia, host of the Second Africa Climate Summit (ACS2) in Addis Ababa in collaboration with the African Union Commission, is working with strategic partners to ensure the summit's success.

H.E. Dr. Fitsum Assefa, Minister of Planning and Development, convened a bilateral meeting with H.E. Alexis Lamek, Ambassador of France to Ethiopia, to explore France's engagement in the upcoming summit.

The discussions underscored the importance of ACS2 for Africa and Ethiopia, with both parties agreeing to collaborate on ensuring a successful and impactful summit.
