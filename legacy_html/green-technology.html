Green Technology

Promoting innovation and excellence in climate mitigation and green development across Ethiopia.

Green Award

The Green Award recognizes outstanding work and innovation in climate change mitigation and green development.

MoPD encourages organizations, innovators, and communities advancing green technology solutions that support Ethiopia's climate-resilient development goals and the Climate Resilient Green Economy (CRGE) strategy.

Through this initiative, the ministry highlights best practices in environmental stewardship, low-carbon innovation, and sustainable growth aligned with national and international climate commitments.
