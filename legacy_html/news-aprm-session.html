The First Extraordinary Session of the African Peer Review Focal Points Steering Committee

The First Extraordinary Session of the African Peer Review Focal Points Steering Committee convened on February 13, 2025, at the Sheraton Hotel in Addis Ababa.

The agenda included reviewing draft selection criteria for appointing APR Panel members and discussing the legal opinion on extending the tenure of current Panel members.

H.E. Dr. Fitsum Assefa opened the session, emphasizing the African Peer Review Mechanism's role in fostering good governance, democracy, and accountability across the continent.

Following productive discussion, both agenda items were addressed and the meeting concluded successfully.
