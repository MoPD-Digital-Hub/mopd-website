#ACS2 Call for Flagship Initiatives Is Now Open

The call for flagship initiatives for the Second Africa Climate Summit (ACS2) is now open. Governments, institutions, organizations, and the private sector are invited to submit impactful, scalable projects aligned with ACS2 themes.

Selected initiatives will be featured in the Flagship Report and Summit sessions in Addis Ababa. Submit your proposal to showcase Africa-led climate solutions on a global stage.
