H.E. Fitsum Assefa

Minister

Fitsum Assefa Adela is a highly accomplished economist and politician who has made significant contributions to Ethiopia's development. She is currently the Minister of Planning and Development, a position she has held since 2018. In this role, she oversees the country's economic planning and development policies.

She earned her undergraduate degree in Accounting and her Master's in Development Studies from Addis Ababa University, and her Doctorate in Agricultural Economics from the University of Giessen in Germany. She worked as a professor at the University of Hawassa for over a decade, conducting research on poverty alleviation, environmental sustainability, and agricultural productivity.

As Minister, she has overseen the implementation of Ethiopia's long-term development plans. She is a strong advocate for evidence-based policymaking and is committed to promoting equality and opportunity for all Ethiopians.
