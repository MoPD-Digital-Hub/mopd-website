Ethiopian Finance Minister Highlights Climate Actions at COP28 UAE

Ethiopian Minister of Finance H.E. Ahmed Shide emphasized the country's commitment to climate action during his opening speech at the Horn of Africa Development Initiative (HODI) panel at COP28 UAE.

He highlighted the government's Climate Resilient Green Economy (CRGE) strategy, the Green Legacy Initiative, and other climate change initiatives, and discussed the impacts of climate change on Ethiopia and the East African region.

The minister called on developed countries to provide more financial and technical support to developing countries to help them address climate change.
